## Remaining Issues

**[P2] Settings modal backdrop still faintly bleeds.** The home content ghosts through the Providers panel. Overlay was deepened 60% -> 70%; a further bump or an opaque scrim would fully kill it. (Some of the reviewer's evidence was pre-bump and overstated this.) Fix: overlay to black/80 or confirm single-layer render. Command: polish SettingsModal/dialog.

**[P2] Home heading voice + vertical centering.** "What's up next?" is cheerful-SaaS and the launcher floats mid-screen (justify-center) in a tall window, off-register for "respect the expert / restraint". Fix: neutral heading, top-align the block, tighten spacing. Command: clarify + layout on HomePage.

**[P3] Provider state taxonomy.** Five overlapping verbs (Connect / Reconnect / Signed in / Add key / Saved key); "Signed in" appears in both subscription and API-key contexts. Fix: distinct vocabulary per axis (subscriptions: Connect/Connected; keys: Add key/Key saved). Command: clarify on ProvidersPanel.

**[P3] Composer/menu accessibility (Sam).** The bespoke contenteditable composer and the @// menus lack listbox/option roles, aria-activedescendant, and chip aria-labels; SR users get no option announcements. The Zed-parity inline-chip model carries a real a11y cost. Fix: add ARIA roles/roving semantics, or reconsider the contenteditable. Command: harden.

**[P3] Small type on elevated surfaces.** 10px uppercase source badges and the `/80` dropdown label sit near the AA floor on card/popover. Fix: bump smallest badges to 11-12px; verify muted-on-card >=4.5:1. Command: typeset/audit.
